Tree cover is tracked through the GIS Tree Tracking pipeline, with each cluster geo-tagged at planting and re-surveyed at six and twelve months. This lets us report **survival**, not just distribution — the metric that actually protects an embankment.

## Why survival, not counts

A sapling handed out is not a tree grown. By tying the household stipend to verified twelve-month survival, the programme aligns incentives with outcomes and produces audit-grade data for partners and funders.

> "The trees on our embankment held during the last storm. That is the difference." — Programme participant, Khulna
